About Chanel

Elegance, innovation, and timeless style since 1910.

Our Story

Founded in 1910 by Gabrielle “Coco” Chanel in Paris, Chanel revolutionized women’s fashion with timeless elegance, simplicity, and innovation.

Today, Chanel continues to lead in luxury fashion, accessories, fragrances, and beauty.

Brand Timeline

1910

Coco Chanel opens her first boutique in Paris.

1921

Launch of the iconic Chanel No. 5 perfume.

Today

Chanel remains a global leader in luxury fashion.
